# Tally Form Spec - English

Use this to duplicate the Russian intake form for the English page. Keep it short; the goal is to receive enough context without asking for sensitive personal data.

## Form Title

Suspicious message review

## Intro Text

Send a suspicious message, job offer, online proposal, or short chat excerpt. I will highlight risk signals, possible template or impersonation signs, verification questions, and a calm reply you can send.

Before submitting, remove or hide personal data: names, phone numbers, addresses, payment details, documents, and account access details.

## Fields

1. **Email for the reply**
   - Type: Email
   - Required: yes

2. **What kind of situation is this?**
   - Type: Multiple choice
   - Required: yes
   - Options:
     - Job offer / employer
     - Freelance client / customer
     - Investment / crypto / quick income
     - Dating / relationship
     - Partnership / business proposal
     - Other

3. **Message or chat text**
   - Type: Long text
   - Required: yes
   - Help text: Paste 1-30 messages. Please remove or replace personal data first.

4. **Who is writing and what are they offering?**
   - Type: Long text
   - Required: yes
   - Help text: Briefly describe the sender: employer, client, partner, acquaintance, unknown account, etc.

5. **What worries you?**
   - Type: Multiple select
   - Required: yes
   - Options:
     - Money / upfront payment / fee
     - Documents / passport / bank card
     - Links / files / account access
     - Too urgent
     - Too generic or templated
     - Possible impersonation
     - Manipulation / pressure
     - I cannot put it into words

6. **Do you need a calm reply you can send?**
   - Type: Multiple choice
   - Required: yes
   - Options:
     - Yes
     - No

7. **Consent to the limits of the review**
   - Type: Checkbox
   - Required: yes
   - Text: I understand that this is not legal advice, a psychological diagnosis, or a guarantee that something is a scam. This is a review of textual risk signals.

## Confirmation Message

Thank you. If the request has enough context, a reply will be sent to the contact email you provided. The first 3 short checks are free for one contact.
