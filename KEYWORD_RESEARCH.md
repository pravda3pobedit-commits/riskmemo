# Keyword Research

Initial public-signal research. Exact search volumes require Google Keyword Planner, Ahrefs, Semrush, Serpstat, or similar tools.

## Core Positioning

Do not lead with "анализ переписки" as the only phrase. Public autocomplete signals suggest stronger demand around concrete anxiety:

- "проверить сообщение"
- "проверить вакансию"
- "проверить работодателя"
- "фейковая вакансия"
- "мошенники удаленная работа"
- "как распознать мошенника"

The English market appears broader around:

- "is this message a scam"
- "is this a scam text message"
- "fake job offer scam"
- "is this job offer a scam"

## Competitor Notes

- `netobmanov.ru` positions around AI message checking.
- `gorodrabot.ru/fraud_vacancy_check` checks vacancy risk.
- English SERP has "is this job offer a scam" style pages and job-offer scam checkers.

Differentiation:

- Manual, human contextual review.
- Not a generic AI score.
- Includes a safe reply the user can send.
- Includes verification questions to force specifics.
- Handles identity-substitution signals: template/script/AI-like text passed off as a real person.

## Monitoring Loop

Weekly:

1. Pull Google/Yandex/Bing autocomplete for 20-30 seed queries.
2. Check SERP for new tools, articles, forums, and competing service pages.
3. Add new questions from Telegram/freelance/remote-work chats.
4. If deployed, inspect GSC queries and impressions.
5. Turn repeated anxiety patterns into case notes or standalone pages.
